Biography

I am a fourth year Ph.D candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Technology (Dept. of CST) at Tsinghua University. I feel lucky to be advised by Prof. Yang Liu. Before that, I received my B.E. degree from the Dept. of CST, Tsinghua, in 2018.

Research Interests

I am interested in many aspects of machine translation:

  • Controllable Generation
  • Uncertainty Estimation and Confidence Calibration
  • Data Augmentation and Pre-Training
  • Multilinguality
